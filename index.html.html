About Us

A Foundation Built on Compassion & Collective Action

InAmigos Foundation (IAF) was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla with a singular vision — to create an India where no child goes uneducated, no woman feels powerless, and no living being suffers in silence.

Operating as a government-licensed Section 8 non-profit and headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we have grown from a small community initiative to a pan-India movement spanning 28 states with over 200 dedicated volunteers.

Our work touches lives across six critical domains — food, education, women's empowerment, animal welfare, environment, and skill development — ensuring a holistic approach to social change.
